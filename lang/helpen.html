  • Tips for a good game

    Our site is very demanding to a good and stable Internet connection, as well as need a modern browser. Recommend:
    1. Use Google Chrome / Opera browsers
    2. Disable video / broadcasts on YouTube / Twitch
    3. Turn off online music
    4. To turn off the broadcast screen Discord / Skype.
    All this increases your ping, and there may be lags

  • Multi-accounts

    It is not allowed, if you use more than 1 account at the same time as it is the way you steal promos for other people. For the use of multiple accounts, some measures may be applied: blocking promotional codes, seizing things.

  • Why skins are being returned to my inventory while withdrawing?

    Probably, your item can not be found at the market, so choose it to another or wait if it appears.

  • Why i can’t use a promo?

    You MUST NOT use two or more accounts on our site. If you do promo codes are blocked for you, but the other functions are still available. If you have only one account, basically, you have already used a promo or the limit has been reached. Also promo codes can be blocked if you send promos in the chat.

  • Why the money that i’ve deposited wasn’t sent to my balance?

    Please, wait for 5-30 minutes, if the problem is not solved, contact support

  • How to get an item using my balance?

    Press the button «Exchange» at the bottom left

  • How to refill the balance?

    You can refill your balance by pressing «Deposit» at the top right.

  • Do you have any social groups?

    Yes, we have VK Group and FB group, where you can find promos and giveaways

  • Promo code?

    Promo codes are posted in our VK group and FB group
